Frequently asked questions

What counts as a message?

A message is any API call to the /messages endpoint, including user input and model response tokens.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. You can change plans at any time from the Console. Prorated charges apply.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, we offer 20% off with annual billing. Contact sales for enterprise agreements.

Is my data used for training?

No. We do not train models on customer data without explicit opt‑in.
